Welcome to ConfidentU

A social media app built to target social anxiety and insecurities. The app comes with features such as a journal, community page, and daily challenges to overcome social anxiety.

What do we do?

WE...

We help you keep track of important tasks to do throughout the day. These tasks can be as simple as brushing your teeth, and making your bed. You can also use it to keep track of other important things, such as chores or work.

Try It Out!

We help you write about your feelings throughout the day using either our free write function, or our many writing prompts. This can be important to keep track of how you feel throughout the day and also to identify your feelings.

We also have a page where you and your community can connect and post together!
